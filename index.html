Bocconi May Workshop on Average-Case Computation

The purpose of this workshop is to explore interactions between topics related to average-case computation: cryptography, average-case complexity, statistical physics, and machine learning. The goal of the workshop is to bring together experts in these areas in order to share recent research and develop connections between these important fields.

The workshop is being co-organized under the European Laboratory for Learning and Intelligent Systems (ELLIS) and the CIFRA Institute.

The workshop is located in the Roentgen building at Bocconi University located at 📍 Via Guglielmo Röntgen 1, 20136 Milano, MI.
